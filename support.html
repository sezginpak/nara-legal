Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my mood?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, select your mood emoji, add activities, and optionally write a note. Tap "Save" to record your entry.

Is my data private?

Yes! Your data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account. We never sell your data to third parties.

How does the AI chat work?

Our AI assistant uses advanced language models to provide supportive conversations and insights based on your mood patterns. Your conversations are processed securely.

What is the Emotion Garden?

The Emotion Garden is a gamification feature where your mood entries help grow virtual plants. Different moods create different types of plants!

How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings > Notifications and enable daily reminders. You can customize the reminder time and notification types.

Can I export my data?

Data export feature is coming soon. Your data is always backed up to iCloud if you enable sync.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through the App Store. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Nara.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently delete all your data from the device and iCloud.
